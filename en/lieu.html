A tropical paradise

 

Perfect for a quiet and peaceful holiday apartments are located opposite the beach "Aveiros" (Portugal, Algarve). There are two swimming pools in the complex, one of which is inside chairs that will be at your disposal and you can shelter from the sun in the shade of palm trees. You can reach the sandy beach via a small path in just 30 seconds. Note that the beach is watch and there is also a restaurant / bar.

 

From the beach "Aveiros" you can reach coves and beaches on the other side.
